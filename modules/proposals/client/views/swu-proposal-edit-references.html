Things to know about your proposed references (click to hide detailsclick to show details)

The Province will require Proponents to support their claims of over-all experience by supplying references who can verify the quality of work provided specific to the relevant experience of the Proponent. Using the upload function below, the Proponent must submit three references setting forth company name, contact name, phone number and email address for each reference. References from the Proponent’s own organization or Team are not acceptable for the Proponent.

The Province may in its sole discretion, but is under no obligation to, check Proponent references without first notifying the Proponent. The Province reserves the right to seek additional references independent of those supplied by the Proponent, including internal references in relation to the Proponent’s performance under any past or current contracts with the Province or other verifications as are deemed necessary by it to verify the information contained in the Proposal and to confirm the suitability of the Proponent.

Further to the Province’s reservation of rights under section 1.21 of the terms and conditions set out on the Terms tab, if the Proponent is deemed unsuitable by the Province in its sole discretion due to any unsatisfactory reference, or if the Proposal is found to contain material errors, omissions or misrepresentations, the Proponent’s proposal will receive no further consideration and the Proponent will be eliminated from the competition.

The Province may only check the lead Shortlisted Proponent’s references but reserves the right to check the references of other Shortlisted Proponents in the event the lead Shortlisted Proponent is deemed unsatisfactory.
