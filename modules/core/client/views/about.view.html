About



At the BCDevExchange, we believe in pursuing a better way of doing things. We ask questions and stay curious. What if developers could work more directly with government? What if we could reduce the paperwork in that relationship? What if we imagined a better way to work and then actually built it together? These are the kinds of questions we explore.

What We’re Changing

We’re building better ways for government and developers to work together. Based on feedback from public sector partners and B.C.’s tech community, we have designed a self-service web app that speeds up how government accesses the software solutions it needs. For developers, it has never been easier or more attractive to work on government projects and improve public services. The pay is fair, the process is open and you can work directly with program managers in GitHub.



How We Got Here

From the beginning, we published the open source code that powers the BCDevExchange app in GitHub, the popular online code sharing platform, and worked openly to share an ever-expanding number of government digital resources, including code, data and Application Program Interfaces (APIs).

Who We Are

We are a team of creative thinkers with diverse backgrounds, some with a deep understanding of how government functions based on years of public service, others with a depth of knowledge and enthusiasm from the private sector.

How You Can Help

We know government doesn’t have a monopoly on good ideas, and experience tells us that collaboration between internal and external partners is the path to uncovering the best ideas. We invite you to see what we are doing, and we welcome your advice and contributions on how to evolve the BCDevExchange. Help us discover better ways of working so that government can do its best work.
