Follow these steps to qualify and apply on Sprint With Us opportunities.

Step 1 - Sign in and claim your capabilities

  1. Sign in to the BCDevExchange with your GitHub account
  2. Claim the capabilities you possess in your personal profile (Settings > My Capabilities & Skills)

Step 2 - Register and qualify your company

Register

To register your company:

  1. Make sure you're signed in
  2. Go to the companies list and click on the + Register a Company button. Complete the form to fill out information about your company.

Qualify

To qualify to apply on Sprint With Us opportunities, your company needs to have at least two members who collectively possess all of these capabilities.

Adding members

To add members, individuals must sign in and claim their capabilities (see Step 1), then find your company in the companies list and click the join button. Once they've done this, when you navigate to your company profile, you'll see their member request and can add them.

Once you have met these qualifications and have accepted the Sprint With UsTerms, you'll be able to submit proposals on Sprint With Us opportunities.
